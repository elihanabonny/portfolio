Sarcasm is Chandler's nickname! If you don't know him he is Chandler Bing, the king of Sarcasm. I love watching clips of his comeback lines. He is a character from Friends which is an American sitcom following the lives of 6 friends living in New York City. We follow them through, their love life, sex life, family moments annd most importantly their friendship. I wouldn't be able to summarize such an amazing sitcom. BlackIsh is an American sitcom following Dre and his family. Calling them iconic is an understatement. From their outfit choices, their family dynamic to how undoubtely smart they are, you will be glued to your TV like a chewing gum under a shoe. They make you laugh while talking about "sensitive subjects" such as racism, colorism, and so on. I feel like there is not enough Black shows or characters accurately represented but this one is a good one. Just like Friends you can watch it and re-watch it do many without getting tired of it!!
